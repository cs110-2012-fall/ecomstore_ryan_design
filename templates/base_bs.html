Meet Michael Collins, Your Quality Jeweler

Michael helps customers bring their jewelry design concepts to reality. Starting with an idea on paper, he produces a wax carving and then casts it in silver, gold or platinum. Michael also loves working with color; he has three people who travel the world to find interesting pieces for him in all hues.

Michael offers:

The best quality diamonds. Diamonds at the best prices. Unmatched service and return policies. The best jewelry guidance and education. In addition to serving as a full-service jeweler, Michael is an excellent corporate citizen. He supports many local organizations, including the East County Boys and Girls Club, Grossmont High School's Partners in Education, Grossmont Hospital Foundation, Soroptimist, La Mesa Rotary, and the Jared Meeker Foundation.
